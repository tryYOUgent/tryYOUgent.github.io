The Basics

So… what is a YOUgent?

A YOUgent is a smart digital concierge built specifically for a business.

It helps customers and employees get answers, find information, and move to the next step — without things falling through the cracks.

Answers Questions

Gives accurate, helpful answers about your business any time someone asks — day or night.

Guides People Forward

Walks customers through their options and moves them to the next step without you lifting a finger.

Saves Information

Captures names, questions, and details automatically — nothing gets lost or forgotten.

Works Around the Clock

Always on. Whether it's 2pm on a Tuesday or 11pm on a Sunday, your YOUgent is ready.
