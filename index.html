The Basics

So… what is a YOUgent?

A YOUgent is a custom AI agent built specifically for your business. It learns your services, your tone, and your workflows — then handles the conversations you don't have time for, around the clock.

Answers Questions

Accurate, consistent answers about your business — any time someone asks.

Guides People Forward

Walks customers through their options and moves them toward the next step — without you having to be there.

Works Around the Clock

2pm Tuesday or 11pm Sunday — it's always on, always ready, and never makes you wait.
