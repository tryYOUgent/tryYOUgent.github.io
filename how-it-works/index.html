The Community Experience

What your congregation and community actually see.

When someone visits your website, sends a message on social media, or reaches out through any connected channel, ChurchCompass is there to meet them. It responds instantly, warmly, and in the voice of your church — not like a bot, but like someone who knows your ministry.

Visitors can ask what to expect on Sunday. Members can submit prayer requests, share personal struggles, or ask follow-up questions about a sermon — all through a natural conversation. No forms. No friction. Just a real exchange.

Every conversation is more than an interaction. Behind the scenes, ChurchCompass is listening, organizing, and capturing structured data from everything that's shared. The community feels heard. Your team gets the information they need.

  • Available 24/7 across website, text, and social channels
  • Responds in the voice and tone of your specific church
  • Captures prayer requests, questions, and care needs automatically
  • Guides visitors through next steps without staff involvement
  • Every interaction feeds your leadership's insight dashboard

The Leadership Experience

Your leadership team. Fully informed. Always ready.

While your community is experiencing ChurchCompass on the front end, your leadership team has their own dedicated interface — built specifically for pastors, care teams, and church administrators.

Instead of opening spreadsheets or scrolling through databases, your team simply asks questions. They can pull up every prayer request submitted this week, check on the status of a specific care need, or ask what topics are coming up most often in community conversations — all through a simple chat.

The internal agent reads your church's data in real time and responds in plain language. It turns your structured backend into a living, breathing insight tool that any staff member can use — no technical training required.

Leadership can update prayer request statuses, flag urgent needs, identify trends, and make ministry decisions grounded in what their congregation is actually experiencing — not what they assume.

  • Ask questions about your congregation in plain language
  • Pull prayer requests, care needs, and trends instantly
  • Update statuses and assign follow-ups through conversation
  • Identify what your community is struggling with right now
  • Make decisions based on real data, not assumptions
