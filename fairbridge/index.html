The Process

One Platform. Three Experiences.

FairBridge handles the entire job fair lifecycle — from vendor registration and payment collection to personalized AI guides for every attendee.

1
⚙️

Organizer Sets Up — Free

Create your event in minutes. Set your vendor booth fee, configure tiers, and share a unique invite link. FairBridge handles everything else — registrations, payments, and payouts.

2
🏢

Vendors Register & Pay

Vendors click the invite link, choose their FairBridge tier (Basic, Enhanced, or Custom Agent), fill out their profile, and pay booth fee + platform fee in one Stripe checkout.

3
🎯

Attendees Get Guided

Every attendee gets a personalized AI guide that knows every vendor, every open role, and every apply link at the fair — plus a full summary email sent after the event.
