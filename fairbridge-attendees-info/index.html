Common Questions

Do I need to download an app? ▾

No. FairBridge works entirely in your browser on any device — phone, tablet, or laptop. Just scan the QR code at the event or visit the link your organizer shares and you're in instantly.

Do I need to create an account? ▾

You just need to provide your name and email when you arrive at the fair so we know where to send your post-fair summary. That's it — no passwords, no lengthy signup.

How does the AI know about the companies at my fair? ▾

Every vendor at a FairBridge-powered event fills out a detailed profile before the fair — their open roles, HR contacts, culture, benefits, and more. The AI reads all of that data so it can answer your questions accurately in real time.

What if my job fair isn't using FairBridge? ▾

You can still use FairBridge at any event that is powered by our platform. If your fair isn't using FairBridge yet, share this page with your organizer — it's free for them to get started.

Is my information shared with the companies at the fair? ▾

Your basic info (name and email) is only shared with vendors you explicitly express interest in — and only if you choose to connect. We never sell or share your data without your permission.
